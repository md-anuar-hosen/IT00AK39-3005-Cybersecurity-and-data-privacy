# Cookie Policy — Booking System
_Last updated: 2025-12-12_

This Cookie Policy explains how the Booking System (“Service”) uses cookies and similar technologies.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small files stored on your device to enhance your experience and support service functionality.

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### A. Essential Cookies (Required)
- Enable login and session management  
- Support security features  
- Cannot be disabled  
- Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f))

### B. Analytics Cookies (Optional)
- Measure usage and performance  
- Activated only with user consent  

### C. Preference Cookies (Optional)
- Store interface and user settings  
- Enable customized user experience

---

## 3. Cookie Consent
Upon visiting the site, you will be shown a cookie banner.  
You can choose to:

- Accept all cookies  
- Reject non-essential cookies  
- Customize your selections  

Consent can be withdrawn at any time.

---

## 4. Managing Cookies
You can remove cookies directly from your browser settings.  
Disabling essential cookies may break login functionality.
